About
NTO Protocol

NTO protocol is conceived to make blockchain data more structured and human-readable.

It simplifies the way we navigate such materials and introduces order, greater transparency and accountability in users.


NTO is used in tandem with a Type II decentralized application / blockchain explorer


operating on the Ethereum network to facilitate user interaction with the protocol. Created for, but not limited to, the crypto-crowdfunding space, this system runs on our native Novum tokens, or NVTs.

Applications




Due Diligence


Complete due diligence and vet authenticity of new projects.

Help community to identify subpar projects.



Curation


Upvote favourite or deserving projects for greater visibility.

More recognition for smaller but promising projects.



Governance and Fund Release


Verify project milestones completion.

Vote on fund disbursements and other events.
